About Us

Welcome to the University Metabolomics Group! We specialize in advanced metabolomics research using state-of-the-art polar HILIC (Hydrophilic Interaction Liquid Chromatography) techniques. Our team is dedicated to exploring the metabolome and providing critical insights into complex biological systems.

Our Analysis Method: Polar HILIC

Our polar HILIC approach is designed to separate and analyze polar metabolites from complex biological samples. This method offers enhanced separation efficiency and sensitivity, which are essential for precise quantification and identification of metabolites in our research.

Our Instruments

  • Orbitrap Exploris 120: An excellent workhorse for routine analyses and method development with high-resolution mass spectrometry.
  • Orbitrap Exploris 240: A cutting-edge instrument offering superior sensitivity and dynamic range for in-depth metabolomic studies.
